Prevent double messaging.
ChannelReply delivers eBay messages via API. Follow these steps to make sure your helpdesk doesn't try to communicate via email at the same time.
  • Log in to eBay and go to “Account settings.”
  • Click “Communication Preferences.”
  • Find "Notification Delivery Format" and click "Show" on the right.
  • Make sure the email address listed in "Send messages to other members" and "Receive messages from other members" does not send messages to or from your helpdesk.
